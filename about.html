ABOUT ME

I've worked for the same, large, Fortune 500 insurance company for the last eleven years. I also never saw myself getting into coding. That all changed when my employer offered a chance to participate in a boot camp and switch careers over to IT as a Developer. I jumped at the chance and here I am!

I participated in, and graduated, General Assembly's 12-week HartCode Web Development Immersive course. Now, I'm a Junior Developer within our Claims Organization. I really like playing with JavaScript and seeing what kind of cool things I can get it to do and I've recently begun learning Java as well.

I'm also a mom and a really big fantasy geek (Game of Thrones, Supernatural, The Magicians, Dungeons & Dragons) so I'm sure I'm raising potential future coders!
